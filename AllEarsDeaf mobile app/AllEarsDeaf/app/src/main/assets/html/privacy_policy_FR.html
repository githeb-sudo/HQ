AllEarsDeaf PRIVACY POLICY (short)

This text is the short version of the privacy policy that governs your use of the software application AllEarsDeaf). 

AllEarsDeaf is a mobile application dedicated to empowering the Deaf Community through equal access to communication. It facilitates communication between the Deaf / Hard of Hearing and the Hearing communities. It provides an automated service that automatically translates American Sign Language and captions speech in real time for immediate communication..

AllEarsDeaf:
  • do not need any registration;
  • do not collect any personal data;
  • do not record any audio (noise indicators are calculated on the fly);
  • needs only basic permissions.
User:
  • can choose (or not) to add his own custom signs to truly communicate on his own terms;
